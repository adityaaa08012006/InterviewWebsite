About ACM PCCoE

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) PCCoE Student Chapter is a vibrant community of computing enthusiasts dedicated to advancing the art, science, and application of computing.

We organize various technical and non-technical events throughout the year to promote innovation, peer learning, and community engagement among students. Our mission is to foster excellence in computing education and research.
